How the NDIS Works

The NDIS helps eligible Australians access supports to live independently. We are here to help you understand your funding options.

We Support All Management Types

Not sure which one you are on? We can help you understand.

Self-Managed

You manage your funding directly. You pay us, and the NDIS reimburses you.

Plan-Managed

A Plan Manager pays your invoices. We send our invoices directly to them.

Agency-Managed

The NDIA manages your funding. (Subject to our current registration/approval status).

Unsure about your funding?

Our team can review your plan with you and explain how to get the most out of your budget.
