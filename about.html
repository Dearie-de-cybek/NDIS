About Phendit Disability & Home Care

We support individuals and families by providing high-quality disability and home care services that promote independence, safety, and wellbeing.

Our Commitment

"Partner in Hope, Every Need Deserves Impactful Touch."

We are committed to safeguarding, dignity, and continuous improvement in everything we do. Our services are delivered with respect, professionalism, and a strong commitment to participant choice and control.

Our Approach

We believe quality care starts with listening—then building support around what matters most to you.

Person-Centred

Goal-focused support tailored to your unique needs.

Collaborative

Transparent partnership with you and your network.

Safe & Ethical

Accountable care that prioritizes your safety.

NDIS Aligned

What Makes Us Different

Our goal is not just to provide support—but to build trust and long-term partnerships.

  • We take time to understand each participant.
  • We focus on outcomes, not just tasks.
  • We prioritise staff consistency and suitability.
  • We maintain strong governance and safeguards.
  • We communicate clearly with participants and families.
